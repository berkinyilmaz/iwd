# Women Who Shaped History

Minimal web app to **discover inspiring women who shaped history** and generate shareable quote cards for Instagram.  
Built to celebrate **International Women's Day** and highlight the impact of remarkable women across history.

No signup.  
No API.  
Just powerful stories and inspiration.

---

## Live Demo

https://iwd-omega.vercel.app/

---


## Features

- **Woman of the Day** – discover a different inspiring woman every day
- **Timeline view** – explore women from ancient history to modern times
- **Favorites system** – save inspiring women to revisit later
- **Random discovery** – instantly explore a random historical figure
- **Detailed profiles** – biography, achievements, and key contributions
- **Quote cards** – generate beautiful shareable quote images
- **Instagram export** – download 1080x1080 quote cards ready for social media
- **Era grouping** – explore women by historical periods
- **Apple-inspired UI** – clean, minimal dark interface
- **Fully responsive design**
- **No authentication**
- **No ads**
- **No tracking**

---

## Tech Stack

- **React (Vite)**
- **Vanilla CSS**
- **Lucide Icons**
- **html-to-image** (quote card export)
- **LocalStorage** (favorites persistence)
- **Vercel** (static deployment)

---

## How It Works

1. Open the app and explore the **Woman of the Day**
2. Browse the **timeline** of influential women
3. Read their **biography, achievements, and quotes**
4. Save inspiring figures to **favorites**
5. Generate a **shareable Instagram quote card**
6. Download the image instantly

> Everything is rendered client-side. Nothing is uploaded.
